About Doug Machado — BMW Master Technician and Founder of DM Performance NJ

Doug Machado is a BMW Master Technician and the founder of DM Performance, a BMW-specialist automotive shop in Wrightstown, New Jersey. He holds BMW's highest technician certification and also builds and competes in BMW race and drift cars.

About Doug Machado and DM Performance

BMW Master Technician Certification

BMW Master Technician certification is the highest level of BMW technical training available. It requires completing BMW's full technical curriculum and demonstrating advanced competency across all BMW vehicle systems. Doug Machado holds this certification, making DM Performance one of the few independent BMW shops in New Jersey operated by a technician at this level. View the full list of services the shop offers.

Race Car Builder and Drift Competitor

In addition to running the shop, Doug Machado builds and competes in BMW race and drift cars. He built his own E46 M3 drift car and has competed at Formula Drift events. This firsthand experience with high-performance BMW builds directly informs the quality of work DM Performance delivers for customer builds — from track preparation to full drift car construction.

Why DM Performance Is BMW-Only

DM Performance exclusively services BMW vehicles. This focus means every tool in the shop, every repair procedure, and every part recommendation is BMW-specific. Customers benefit from a technician whose expertise is concentrated entirely on one brand — rather than spread across dozens of different vehicle makes. Learn more about the shop on the homepage.

New Jersey BMW Community

Doug Machado has built a following of over 14,000 BMW enthusiasts on social media through his documentation of builds, repairs, and race events. DM Performance Location and Service Area

DM Performance is based in Wrightstown, New Jersey and serves BMW owners throughout the state. The shop handles BMWs from all models and generations, from classic E-series chassis to current G-series vehicles.
