BMW Repair and Performance Shop in New Jersey — DM Performance

DM Performance is a BMW-only automotive shop in Wrightstown, New Jersey, operated by BMW Master Technician Doug Machado. The shop handles the full range of BMW services, from routine oil changes and brake jobs to complete engine rebuilds and race car builds.

About DM Performance BMW Specialist

What Makes DM Performance Different from Other Shops

Most auto shops service multiple brands. DM Performance services one: BMW. That single-brand focus means every technician, every tool, and every procedure in the shop is optimized for BMW specifically. Customers get dealership-level expertise without dealership pricing. Learn more on the About page or view our full list of services.

BMW Master Technician Certification

Doug Machado holds BMW's Master Technician certification — the highest technical qualification BMW awards. This certification requires passing advanced training modules and demonstrating competency across all BMW systems including engines, drivetrains, electrical, and emissions. Very few independent shops in New Jersey have a technician at this level.

BMW Maintenance and Repair Services in NJ

DM Performance provides complete BMW maintenance services: oil and filter changes, brake pad and rotor replacement, cooling system repair, VANOS service, timing chain service, wheel bearing replacement, and electrical diagnostics. Whether your BMW needs a routine service or a complex repair, the shop handles it with the same level of care and expertise.

BMW Performance Upgrades

For BMW owners who want more from their cars, DM Performance installs and configures performance upgrades including coilover suspension systems, exhaust upgrades, cold air intakes, ECU tuning, and big brake kits. All upgrades are installed by BMW-trained technicians who understand how modifications interact with BMW-specific systems. See the full performance service list.

BMW Engine Rebuilds and Engine Swaps

DM Performance performs full BMW engine rebuilds and engine swaps, including popular swap configurations using BMW S54, S65, B58, and N54 engines. Engine work includes rod bearing service, head gasket replacement, valve cover service, and comprehensive oil system repairs. The shop handles engine work that many general repair shops decline to take on.

BMW Race Car and Drift Car Builds

Doug Machado builds race cars in addition to servicing customer vehicles. He has built and competed in his own E46 M3 drift car and has attended Formula Drift events. This hands-on racing experience directly informs how DM Performance approaches performance builds for customer BMWs — including track preparation, alignment, corner balancing, and custom fabrication work.
