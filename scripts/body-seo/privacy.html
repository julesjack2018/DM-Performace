Privacy Policy for DM Performance LLC — BMW Specialist in New Jersey

This is the privacy policy for DM Performance LLC, a BMW specialist automotive shop located in Wrightstown, New Jersey. It explains how personal information submitted through dmperformancenj.com is collected and used.

About This Privacy Policy

Information Collected

DM Performance collects personal information when users submit contact forms or appointment requests, including name, email, phone number, and vehicle details. Basic website usage data may also be collected through analytics tools.

How Information Is Used

Information is used solely to respond to service inquiries and schedule BMW service appointments. DM Performance does not sell personal data to third parties. For questions, contact info@dmperformancenj.com. Book a service appointment or view the services page.
