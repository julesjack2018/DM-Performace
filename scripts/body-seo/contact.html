Book a BMW Service Appointment at DM Performance — Wrightstown, NJ

DM Performance accepts BMW service appointments by online form, phone, and email. The shop is located in Wrightstown, New Jersey and is operated by BMW Master Technician Doug Machado.

Contacting DM Performance for BMW Service

How to Book an Appointment

Appointments can be requested by filling out the online booking form on this page, calling or texting 732-865-0566, or emailing info@dmperformancenj.com. The shop will confirm the appointment directly. For fastest response, calling or texting during business hours is recommended.

Phone and Text

DM Performance can be reached by phone or text at 732-865-0566. This is the fastest way to get a response for urgent service needs or quick questions about your BMW.

Email

For non-urgent inquiries, quotes, or detailed service questions, email info@dmperformancenj.com. Include your vehicle year, model, mileage, and a description of the service needed for the fastest response.

Shop Location

DM Performance is located in Wrightstown, New Jersey. Exact address is provided at time of appointment confirmation. The shop serves BMW owners throughout New Jersey including Trenton, Toms River, Freehold, and surrounding areas.

What to Expect

After submitting a request, DM Performance will contact you to confirm the appointment and provide any additional information needed. The shop provides written estimates before beginning non-emergency work. Learn about the full range of services on the services page or read about the team on the about page.

Online Booking System

DM Performance uses a third-party appointment scheduling platform for online bookings. This system allows customers to select services and available times. Appointments booked online are subject to confirmation by the shop.
